Academic Interests

While studying at Chico State, I delved into the petrology and geochemistry of local Iron-Oxide-Copper-Gold deposits, and I was fortunate to present my research at the 2024 GSA Conference in Spokane, WA. In May, I earned my Bachelor’s Degree in Geology, and I’m currently expanding my skills by pursuing a GIS certificate from Cal Poly Humboldt.

Work Experience

I am currently working as a Geologist at Luhdorff & Scalmanini, Consulting Engineers, a consulting firm that specializes in sustainable management of groundwater resources. At LSCE I make a living through creating GIS maps, monitoring groundwater levels and water quality, tracking aquifer recharge, and compiling my findings into technical reports for use by several counties across Northern California.
